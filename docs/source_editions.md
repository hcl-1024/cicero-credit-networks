# Primary-source editions consulted

The local source scans used to check the paper are the following Loeb Classical Library editions. Original publication years and the consulted reprint are distinguished where the scan records both.

- Cicero, *Letters to Atticus*, translated by E. O. Winstedt: volume I (1912; 1919 reprint consulted), volume II (1913), and volume III (1918; 1961 reprint consulted).
- Cicero, *Letters to His Friends*, translated by W. Glynn Williams: volume I (1927; 1958 reprint consulted) and volume II (1928; 1952 reprint consulted).
- Cicero, *Letters to His Brother Quintus*, translated by W. Glynn Williams, and *Letters to Brutus*, translated by M. Cary, in *Letters to His Friends*, volume III (1929; revised 1954; 1960 reprint consulted).
- Plutarch, *Lives*, translated by Bernadotte Perrin: volume III, including *Crassus* (1916); volume V, including *Pompey* (1917); volume VII, including *Caesar* (1919); volume VIII, including *Cato the Younger* (1919); and volume IX, including *Antony* (1920).

These descriptions identify the editions used for verification; they do not imply that copyrighted source text is redistributed in this repository.
